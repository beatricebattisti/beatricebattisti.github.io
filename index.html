Beatrice Battisti

I am a postdoctoral fellow at CNRS, Université Savoie Mont Blanc in Chambéry (France).

I currently work on the design, analysis and implementation of novel numerical schemes for multi-phase flows, for volcanic applications.

My research interest revolves around applying numerical schemes to partial differential equations to describe the physics of environmental phenomena. In particular, I am interested in numerical modeling of multi-phase flows and implementing model order reduction methods for non-linear problems.
